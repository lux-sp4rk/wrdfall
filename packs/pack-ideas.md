# Word Loom — Theme Pack Ideas

Theme packs should feel warm and familiar (not internet-meme-y).
Each pack: ~50–150 puzzles.

## Pack ideas (senior-friendly)
1. **Cooking & Kitchen** — ingredients, tools, meals
2. **Classic Movies** — genres, roles, famous terms (keep it general)
3. **Garden & Nature** — plants, birds, weather
4. **Travel & Places** — common destinations/terms
5. **Family & Home** — household words, relationships
6. **Music & Radio** — instruments, eras, listening words

## Rules to keep themes safe
- Avoid slang-heavy themes.
- Avoid anything that feels political.
- Favor nouns and simple verbs.

## Monetization packaging
- Base game includes a “Sampler” of each theme.
- Packs are one-time purchases.
- Optional: “Annual pass” for all packs.
