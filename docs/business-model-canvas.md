# Business Model Canvas: Wordfall

**Date:** 2026-02-11
**Version:** 1.0
**Project:** Wordfall (Seniors-First Word Puzzle)

---

## 1. Value Propositions
*Why will people buy this?*
- **The Anti-Casino:** A calm, respectful digital space. No ads, no timers, no flashing lights, no "energy" meters.
- **Cognitive Clarity:** Large, high-contrast UI designed specifically for aging eyes and motor control (large tap targets).
- **Ownership:** "Buy it once, own it forever." A respectful transaction.
- **Curated Content:** Hand-crafted thematic puzzles (e.g., "Grandma's Garden", "Kitchen Traditions") that evoke nostalgia, unlike algorithmic gibberish.

## 2. Customer Segments
*Who are we helping?*
- **Primary:** Seniors (65+) looking for mental stimulation without anxiety or tech frustration.
- **Secondary (The Purchasers):** Adult children (35-55) buying a "safe" digital gift for their aging parents (the "Digital Hearth" gift).
- **Tertiary:** Activity Directors at senior living communities / nursing homes.

## 3. Channels
*How do we reach them?*
- **App Stores (iOS/Android):** Optimized search for "ad-free word game", "senior games", "large print puzzle".
- **Direct Gift:** "Gift this App" functionality on iOS.
- **B2B Outreach:** Direct email/contact with Activity Directors at senior centers (bulk codes).
- **Content Marketing:** Blog posts on "Digital calming", "Games for cognitive health".

## 4. Customer Relationships
*How do we interact?*
- **Trust-First:** Zero data harvesting. Privacy policy is one sentence: "We don't track you."
- **Direct Support:** A human email address for help (no chatbots).
- **Community:** Potential "Daily Puzzle" that everyone plays (async connection).

## 5. Revenue Streams
*How do we make money?*
- **Premium Upfront:** $4.99 one-time purchase (Base Game).
- **Expansion Packs:** $1.99 thematic DLCs (Theme Packs).
- **"Master Weaver" Pass:** $14.99 bundle (All current + future content).
- **Institutional Licenses:** Bulk pricing for senior centers.

## 6. Key Resources
*What do we need?*
- **Godot Engine:** Cross-platform development.
- **Puzzle Content:** High-quality, culturally relevant word lists (not just dictionary scrapes).
- **Brand Trust:** A reputation for not exploiting the user.

## 7. Key Activities
*What must we do?*
- **Development:** Polish the "Loom Drop" mechanic and accessibility features.
- **Curation:** Authoring thematic word lists.
- **Marketing:** Positioning the game as a "digital sanctuary."

## 8. Key Partners
*Who helps us?*
- **Platform Holders:** Apple/Google (App Store distribution).
- **Senior Living Networks:** Potential distribution partners.
- **Content Creators:** Possibility to hire crossword authors for specialty packs.

## 9. Cost Structure
*What are the costs?*
- **App Store Fees:** 15-30% commission.
- **Development Time:** Engineering & Design.
- **Marketing/Assets:** Store art, trailer, potentially ads (ironically).

---

## Strategic Angle: "The Digital Hearth"
Positioning Wordfall not just as a game, but as a piece of **furniture** for the digital life of a senior—reliable, sturdy, and respectful.
