# How to Play Wordfall

Wordfall is a relaxing word puzzle with a twist: letters fall like rain, and you've got to spell words before the board fills up!

## The Basics

1. **The board starts with 3 rows of letters** — including some seed words to get you going
2. **New letters fall** one at a time from the top into the 5×5 grid
3. **Swipe across touching letters** to spell a word (3 letters or longer)
4. **Matched letters disappear** — everything above falls down to fill the gaps
5. **Keep the board clear** to win. If it fills completely, game over!

## Making Words

Drag your finger (or mouse) across letters that are **next to each other**. You can go:
- Up, down, left, right
- Diagonally (all four corners count!)

Each letter must touch the previous one. No jumping!

**Tip:** Longer words = bigger scores. A 5-letter word is worth *way* more than two 3-letter words.

## Scoring

Your score for each word = **Letter Sum × Length Multiplier × Combo Multiplier**

**1. Letter Rarity**
Common letters (A, E, S, T, I, L, N, O, R, U) are worth 1 point. Rare ones (J, Q, X, Z) are worth 8-10 points.

**2. Word Length**
The longer your word, the more your score multiplies:
- 3 letters: 1× (base score)
- 4 letters: 2×
- 5 letters: 4×
- 6+ letters: 8×

**3. Combo Streak**
Chain together 4-letter words (or longer) to build a streak. Each one adds +0.5× to your combo multiplier:
- 1 in a row: 1.5×
- 2 in a row: 2.0×
- 3 in a row: 2.5×
- 4+ in a row: 3.0× (cap)

⚠️ **Watch out:** A 3-letter word breaks your streak completely!

**Score examples:**
| Word | Letters | Sum | Length | Combo | Total |
|------|---------|-----|--------|-------|-------|
| CAT | C=3, A=1, T=1 | 5 | 1× | 1.0× | **5** |
| STAR | S=1, T=1, A=1, R=1 | 4 | 2× | 1.0× | **8** |
| QUEST | Q=10, U=1, E=1, S=1, T=1 | 14 | 4× | 1.0× | **56** |
| JAZZ | J=8, A=1, Z=10, Z=10 | 29 | 2× | 1.0× | **58** |

With a 2-word streak (2.0× combo), STAR would score: 4 × 2 × 2.0 = **16**

## Speeding Up

The pace gradually increases:
- Every **5 letters dropped**, the speed picks up a notch
- A **5+ letter word** resets the speed back to nice and slow — think of it as hitting the "calm down" button
- The fastest it gets is a 2-second interval (speed floor)

## Power-Ups

Earn points by clearing words, then spend them on helpful tools. Each power-up gets slightly more expensive the more you use it!

### 🔄 Shake
Shuffles all letters into new random spots. Great when you're stuck!
- **Normal:** starts at 3 pts | **Hard:** starts at 8 pts

### 🔀 Swap
Pick any two letters and trade places. Perfect for setting up that big word.
- **Normal:** starts at 2 pts | **Hard:** starts at 5 pts

*Tap Swap, tap your first letter, tap your second letter. Press ESC to cancel.*

### ⬇️ Draw More
Drops up to 5 new letters onto the board. Use when you're running out of options.
- **Normal:** starts at 5 pts | **Hard:** starts at 10 pts

### ⏸️ Freeze
Pauses all letter drops. Resume anytime for free!
- **Normal:** starts at 10 pts | **Hard:** starts at 15 pts

## Difficulty Modes

**Normal mode** is the relaxed default — more vowels, rescue words help when you're stuck, and lower power-up costs.

**Hard mode** is for when you want a challenge — faster drops (4 seconds instead of 8), fewer vowels, no rescue words, and steeper power-up costs.

## Win & Lose

✅ **You win** when every tile is empty. Clear the whole board!

❌ **You lose** when all 25 spaces fill up — no room for the next letter.

**Stuck with no words?** Try a power-up! In Normal mode, the game will occasionally drop letters that help you find words.

## Tips for Success

- **Build downward** — clearing letters at the bottom makes everything above fall, creating new word chances
- **Save your streak** — one big word is usually better than two small ones
- **Use power-ups early** — don't wait until the board is almost full
- **Look for common endings** — ING, ED, ER, and ES turn short words into long ones

## Settings

**Language:** Switch between English and Spanish anytime. Your high scores are saved separately for each.

**Difficulty:** Choose Normal for a relaxed experience or Hard for extra challenge.

**Sound & Music:** Toggle in the menu. The gentle rainfall sound can be quite soothing!

---

*Relax, take your time, and enjoy weaving words. There's no timer counting down — just a gentle rhythm of falling letters and the satisfaction of a well-placed word.*
