# How to Play Word Loom

Word Loom is a relaxing word puzzle with a twist: letters fall like rain, and you've got to spell words before the board fills up!

## The Basics

1. **Letters fall** one at a time into the grid
2. **Swipe across touching letters** to spell a word (3 letters or longer)
3. **Matched letters disappear** — everything above falls down to fill the gaps
4. **Keep the board clear** to win. If it fills completely, game over!

## Making Words

Drag your finger (or mouse) across letters that are **next to each other**. You can go:
- Up, down, left, right
- Diagonally (all four corners count!)

Each letter must touch the previous one. No jumping!

**Tip:** Longer words = bigger scores. A 5-letter word is worth *way* more than two 3-letter words.

## Scoring

Points come from three things:

**1. Letter Rarity**  
Common letters (A, E, S, T) are worth 1 point. Rare ones (J, Q, X, Z) are worth 8-10 points.

**2. Word Length**  
The longer your word, the more your score multiplies:
- 3 letters: normal points
- 4 letters: double points
- 5 letters: 4× points
- 6+ letters: 8× points!

**3. Streak Bonus**  
Chain together 4-letter words (or longer) to build a streak. Each one in a row adds to your bonus multiplier — up to 3× your score!

⚠️ **Watch out:** A 3-letter word breaks your streak. Sometimes it's worth it, sometimes it's not!

## Speeding Up

The game gradually gets faster as you play. Every few letters, they fall a bit quicker.

**Good news:** Spell a 5+ letter word and the speed resets to nice and slow. Think of long words as hitting the "calm down" button.

## Power-Ups

Earn points by clearing words, then spend them on helpful tools:

### 🔄 Mix Up (Shake)
**Cost:** 3 points  
Shuffles all letters into new random spots. Great when you're stuck!

### 🔀 Swap
**Cost:** 2 points  
Pick any two letters and trade places. Perfect for setting up that big word.

*Tap Swap, tap your first letter, tap your second letter. Done!*

## Win & Lose

✅ **You win** when every tile is empty. Clear the whole board!

❌ **You lose** when all 25 spaces fill up — no room for the next letter.

**Stuck with no words?** Try a power-up! The game will also occasionally drop helpful letters when you're in a tight spot.

## Tips for Success

- **Build downward** — clearing letters at the bottom makes everything above fall, creating new word chances
- **Save your streak** — one big word is usually better than two small ones
- **Use power-ups early** — don't wait until the board is almost full
- **Look for common endings** — ING, ED, ER, and ES turn short words into long ones

## Settings

**Language:** Switch between English and Spanish anytime. Your high scores are saved separately for each.

**Sound & Music:** Toggle in the menu. The gentle rainfall sound can be quite soothing!

---

*Relax, take your time, and enjoy weaving words. There's no timer counting down — just a gentle rhythm of falling letters and the satisfaction of a well-placed word.*
