# Word Loom — Starter 10 Puzzles (v0)

Format:
- Tray: letters available (use each at most once unless stated)
- Slot: length + rule
- Answer: one valid solution (not necessarily unique)

## 1) Warm-up
- Tray: S T A R E N
- Slot: 4 letters
- Answer: EARN

## 2) Starts-with
- Tray: S T A R E N
- Slot: 5 letters, starts with S
- Answer: STARE

## 3) Ends-with
- Tray: C A R E T P
- Slot: 5 letters, ends with E
- Answer: TRACE

## 4) Contains
- Tray: B R A I N S
- Slot: 5 letters, must include RA
- Answer: BRAIN

## 5) Cozy nouns
- Tray: G A R D E N
- Slot: 6 letters
- Answer: GARDEN

## 6) Gentle constraint
- Tray: L I S T E N
- Slot: 6 letters, ends with N
- Answer: LISTEN

## 7) Slightly harder
- Tray: C H A I R S
- Slot: 6 letters, starts with C
- Answer: CHAIRS

## 8) Contains digraph
- Tray: S H A R E D
- Slot: 5 letters, must include SH
- Answer: SHARE

## 9) Longer
- Tray: P L A N E T S
- Slot: 7 letters
- Answer: PLANETS

## 10) Two-slot (first multi)
- Tray: S T O N E R
- Slot A: 4 letters, starts with S
- Slot B: 4 letters, ends with E
- Example answers:
  - A: SONE (alt: SORE)
  - B: TONE

Notes:
- Puzzle 10 intentionally allows multiple solutions; in v0 we should accept any valid word meeting rules.
- For senior-friendliness, keep trays readable (avoid rare letters early).
